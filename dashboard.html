Why Data and Statistics can help us track the Virus?

In the process of surviellance of an infectious disease, data and statistics provide important information on the spread of Sars Cov-2 infecting individuals across the globe with coronavirus disease. Using real-time data to track new cases on a daily bases helps us to understand the extent to which the virus is spreading, not only across whole countries but within different regions and areas of cities,towns and local communities.
