Becerra-Flores et al have highlighted a mutation of an Aspartate to a Glycine at position 614 in the spike protein that has strong correlation to increased mortality rate. It is thought that a Glycine in this position alters the protein’s conformation making the entire protein more accessible to the relevant receptors.
